GW CS Senior Design 2016

Saurabh Singh : Peer Perspective

Peer perspective goal is to have students be capable of identifying misconceptions that lead to incorrect solutions. to further their understanding. To do this students are given: A topic list where they are asked to select a preferential topic. Randomly generated problems accompanied by their incorrect solutions. Step by step procedure generated by Wolfram Alpha. An incremental difficulty with progression.

Bio: Saurabh Singh a graduating senior getting a B.S. in computer science and minor in Psychology. I came into GWU not knowing what I wanted to do with a computer science degree. But now slowly I've started to realize what I want to do. I had a interest in Psychology as well as computer science so I hope I can go into an area where they two meet.
